Why the name Vayzip?

What does “Vayzip” mean?
“Vay” comes from vacay — vacation. “Zip” stands for speed and simplicity. The goal is straightforward: help you find vacation rentals faster.
What makes it different?
Instead of forcing you through endless lists, Vayzip starts with the map and keeps search fast. You can explore areas visually, then narrow down to the right stay with practical filters.
When can I try it?
Soon. We’re shipping iteratively — starting small, then expanding destinations and capabilities as the product proves itself.
